About Us

GK Pickles brings the authentic taste of traditional Andhra homemade pickles to your family.

At GK Pickles, every batch is prepared using carefully selected ingredients, traditional recipes, and homemade methods passed down through generations. We believe great pickles should taste just like they do at home: fresh, flavorful, and made with care.

We use quality fruits, vegetables, spices, and cold-pressed oils to create rich, authentic flavors that complement every meal. From spicy Avakaya to tangy Gongura, every jar is packed with traditional Andhra taste.
